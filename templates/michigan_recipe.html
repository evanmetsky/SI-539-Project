The Cowboy Curtis From The Fly Trap

The Fly Trap is not your granddaddy’s diner: The inside is sleek, the soundtrack is ska and reggae, and the breakfast is, well, it’s called “blunch”: hearty entrees with a breakfast twist. One prime example is the Cowboy Curtis, a hefty cut of steak served with two fried eggs, garlic potatoes and toast. Thick “Wildwest” sauce (it’s like ketchup with a kick) ties it all together. 22950 Woodward Ave.
